physics.TemperatureSolver¶

Temperature equation solver.

Uses scipy’s sparse arrays and sparse direct matrix solver to solve the temperature equation in 2D.

Functions

TemperatureSolver(tstep, xnum, ynum, gridx, ...)

Formulates and solves the heat conservation eqn on a 2D, irregularly spaced grid using scipy sparse matricies and solver.
